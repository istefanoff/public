Simple text-based dungeon game, where you choose commands and attack monsters.

You start with 100 health.
You do and get random damage.
You can attack or run away.
You can drink a potion if you have any.
Each monster you kill gives you 1 gold.
Monsters have a chance to drop a potion.
You can buy a potion for 5 gold.
If you run out of health, you lose.
